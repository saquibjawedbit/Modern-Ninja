The Modern Ninja is an action- adventure 3D platformer game with mixed RPG elements. In this game, you play the role of modern ninja, who is the strongest ninja of his generation who use lightning as his weapons. In the time of crisis, The modern ninja is given a mission to find the way and close the demons realm gate. High quality graphics and smooth gameplay. A single-player offline game. Come join us and become the savior of the ninja world. Travel afar underground. Manuever through traps, stop anyone who comes on your way and save the humanity.
